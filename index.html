Job Summary

The Human AI Logistics Mediator plays a vital role in supporting Canada's next-generation logistics infrastructure by enabling seamless coordination between AI systems, robotic operations, and real-world environments. This position offers the opportunity to participate in advanced remote work supported by cutting-edge augmented reality (AR) technology, allowing individuals to contribute from flexible locations while remaining connected to national systems.

Human AI Logistics Mediators support operational continuity by interpreting system prompts, validating automated decisions, and responding to real-time logistics needs. The role emphasizes adaptability, technological fluency, and the ability to work across dynamic conditions. As part of a distributed workforce, mediators contribute to efficient, scalable operations that support economic resilience and innovation.

This position is well suited to individuals who are comfortable working with emerging technologies, value flexibility, and are interested in participating in the evolving future of work.

  • Maintain continuous oversight of AI-directed logistics systems through approved AR/MR interfaces during assigned shifts
  • Interpret and respond to real-time system prompts, alerts, and task reallocations to ensure uninterrupted operations
  • Monitor AI and robotic system performance, identifying errors, deviations, or environmental constraints requiring human intervention
  • Execute physical and digital tasks under remote instruction to support automated workflows and resolve operational exceptions
  • Track, verify, and log task completion, system activity, and performance metrics using live dashboards
  • Comply with monitoring, documentation, and behavioral assessment protocols throughout all assignments
